# AiTerm Documentation

This is the main documentation repository for the AiTerm Chrome extension. 
The product description, technical requirements and architectural solutions are stored here.

## About the project
AiTerm is a smart, AI-powered Google Chrome extension designed for deep vocabulary learning, mastering phrases, and understanding complex terms. 🧠✨

Unlike standard translators, AiTerm leverages advanced neural networks to focus on the specific vocabulary you want to truly understand and remember. It’s the ultimate tool for anyone preparing for exams like IELTS or TOEFL, aiming to expand their word bank, and striving to feel the language like a native speaker. 🌍

🔥 What You Get Beyond Translation:
📊 CEFR Difficulty Levels (A1 to C2): Instantly see the level of a word to know if it matches your current learning goals.

📈 Usage Frequency: Learn how popular a word is among native speakers. Decide whether it’s worth your time to master or if you can skip it.

📖 Real-Life Examples: Read authentic sentences to grasp the correct context immediately.

✨ Rich Synonyms: Enrich your speech! Discover related words and expand your vocabulary in a single search.

🤖 Detailed AI Explanations: Get more than just a dry translation. Our AI analyzes the essence and explains the nuances of every term for deep comprehension.

You can save new words to your Personal Library. Create custom dictionaries that include not only translations and transcriptions but the full context for maximum retention! 📚✅
